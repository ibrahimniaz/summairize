About SummAIrise

Built for people who care about staying informed — not drowning in it.

Research shows the average American is exposed to around 100,000 words of information every day across all media — TV, radio, internet, and print. Most of it is noise: repeated stories, algorithmically-ranked outrage, clickbait headlines. Yet staying genuinely informed still matters.

SummAIrise cuts through it. You choose the sources you trust and the topics you care about. Our AI reads everything published in your time window, filters out the irrelevant, and writes you a sharp, well-sourced digest — in the time it takes to make a coffee.

No algorithmic feed. No clickbait. No ads. Just the news you actually want, synthesised from outlets like BBC News, Reuters, The Guardian, Al Jazeera, AP News, and more.

We're a small, independent product built in London. We'd love to hear your feedback.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is SummAIrise free to use?
+
Yes — SummAIrise has a free tier that gives you 1 digest per day with up to 2 sources and 2 topics. For unlimited digests, access to all 11 sources, all tones and lengths, and keyword focus, you can top up with credits starting from £0.99. Credits never expire.
What sources does SummAIrise draw from?
+
We pull from 11 live news sources: BBC News, Reuters, The Guardian, Al Jazeera, AP News, Sky News, CNN, Deutsche Welle, NPR News, France 24, and euronews. All sources are updated in real time via their official RSS feeds.
How recent are the articles in my digest?
+
You choose the time window: 6 hours (breaking news), 24 hours (today's stories), or 72 hours (broader picture). Free tier users get 24 hours only; the 6h and 72h windows are available with credits. The AI only includes articles published within your chosen window, so your digest is always current.
What is a credit?
+
One credit = one digest. Credits unlock the full experience: all 11 sources, all 9 topics, all tones and lengths, keyword focus, and the 6h and 72h time windows. Credits come in packs: 5 for £0.99, 20 for £2.99, 60 for £6.99, or 150 for £14.99. They never expire and you're only charged when you generate a digest.
Is my digest actually personalised?
+
Yes. Every digest is generated fresh for you, based on your exact choices of sources, topics, tone, time window, and keywords. There are no pre-written templates — the AI reads the live articles and writes your digest from scratch each time.
What AI model does SummAIrise use?
+
Free tier digests use a fast, capable AI model. Credit-based (premium) digests use a more powerful model that produces richer analysis, better sourcing, and more nuanced writing.
Can I get a refund?
+
Under UK consumer law, you have a 14-day cancellation right for digital purchases. However, by using credits immediately you waive this right for consumed credits. Unused credits can be refunded within 14 days — just email contact@summairise.com and we'll sort it.
How is SummAIrise different from a news aggregator?
+
News aggregators show you a list of links. SummAIrise reads the articles and writes you a synthesised digest — like having a well-read friend brief you on the day's news. You get analysis, context, sourced quotes, and footnotes, not just headlines.

8. Third-party processors. We use Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), and Anthropic (AI). Each operates under their own privacy policies.

9. Contact. For any privacy questions, email contact@summairise.com.
